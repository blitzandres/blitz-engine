# Blitz Engine — Prohibited Uses

The following uses are **explicitly prohibited** by the Apache 2.0 license terms, our ethics framework, and applicable law (including EU AI Act 2024/1689).

Using Blitz Engine for any of these purposes is a violation of the license.

---

## Absolutely Prohibited

### Employment & Hiring
- Screening job applicants
- Monitoring employee behavior or performance
- Making employment decisions based on Blitz Engine output

### Law Enforcement & Legal
- Criminal investigations
- Court evidence or expert testimony
- Parole or probation assessment
- Immigration or border control screening

### Healthcare & Mental Health
- Medical diagnosis
- Mental health assessment
- Insurance risk scoring
- Clinical decision support

### Education
- Student assessment or discipline
- Cheating detection
- Classroom behavioral surveillance

### Financial Services
- Loan applications
- Insurance underwriting
- Fraud investigation as sole evidence

### Without Consent
- Analyzing any person who has not explicitly consented
- Covert surveillance
- Analyzing recorded footage without subjects' knowledge

---

## High-Risk Uses (Require Human Review and Expertise)

These uses are not prohibited but carry significant risk. A human expert must review all outputs and Blitz Engine must not be the sole basis for any decision:

- Journalism investigation (context only, never sole evidence)
- Academic research on real subjects (requires IRB approval)
- Security research with consented volunteers

---

## Permitted Uses

- Academic research with appropriate ethics approval
- Educational demonstrations of behavioral analysis
- Personal use on consented subjects
- Development and testing with synthetic or consented data
- Journalism — as one contextual signal among many, never as evidence

---

## Enforcement

Violations of these prohibited uses will result in:
1. License termination
2. Removal of access to any hosted Blitz Engine services
3. Public disclosure if violations cause harm to individuals
